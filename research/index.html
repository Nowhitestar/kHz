研究内容

The pursuit of new concepts in catalysis: organocatalysis, cascade, synergistic, photoredox and metallaphotoredox catalysis and the application of such to the total synthesis of natural products and pharmaceuticals.

Photoredox Catalysis

The use of visible light to enable single-electron transfer between photoexcitable catalysts and organic molecules or transition metal catalysts via modulation of oxidation states.

Princeton & Merck

Check out our Photoredox tab for tips and tricks on how to run our reactions. We are currently collaborating with Merck on a photoreactor, a design that speeds up reaction times and standardizes parameters.
